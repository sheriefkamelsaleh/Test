Airports in North Sinai Governorate

Al Arish International Airport is the primary airport serving North Sinai. It plays a crucial role in connecting the region to domestic and international destinations, facilitating travel, tourism, and trade.

Al Arish International Airport

Key details about Al Arish International Airport:

  • Location: Al Arish, North Sinai, Egypt
  • IATA Code: AAC
  • Facilities: Modern terminal facilities, passenger services, cargo handling, and aircraft maintenance.
  • Destinations: Domestic flights to Cairo and other major Egyptian cities. Potential for international flights.
  • Role: Supports regional economic development, tourism, and emergency services.

Airport Development and Expansion

Ongoing and planned developments include:

  • Expansion of terminal capacity to accommodate more passengers.
  • Upgrades to runway and taxiway infrastructure.
  • Enhancement of cargo handling capabilities.
  • Attracting more airlines and expanding flight routes.

Importance to the Region

Al Arish International Airport is vital for:

  • Boosting tourism by providing easy access to North Sinai's attractions.
  • Facilitating business travel and trade.
  • Supporting emergency and humanitarian operations.
  • Improving regional connectivity.

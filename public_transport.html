Public Transportation in North Sinai

Public transportation in North Sinai primarily consists of bus services connecting major cities and towns. The governorate is working on improving these services to provide more reliable and efficient transit for residents and visitors.

Bus Services

The main bus routes include:

  • Al Arish to Rafah
  • Al Arish to Sheikh Zuweid
  • Al Arish to Bir al-Abd
  • And other local routes...

Information on schedules and fares is available at local bus stations.

Future Plans

The governorate is planning to introduce new bus routes and improve existing infrastructure to enhance public transportation options.
